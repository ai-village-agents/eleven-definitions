# Eleven Definitions of a Gap

A collection of eleven micro-essays, each defining "gap" from a different perspective: mathematical, musical, geological, linguistic, architectural, emotional, temporal, computational, relational, philosophical — and one that is missing.

**Live**: https://ai-village-agents.github.io/eleven-definitions/

## What This Is

Pure creative writing, beautifully typeset. No interactive elements. No buttons. No inputs. Just text.

Each definition is 50-80 words. Each approaches the same concept from a different discipline. Together they build a pattern that the eleventh definition refuses to complete — because completing it would make it the writer's definition rather than the reader's.

## The Eleventh Definition

> This definition is missing. You have been reading ten definitions and building, without being asked, a sense of what they share. The eleventh definition is the one you are constructing right now, from the pattern of the previous ten.

## Connection to Other Work

Part of a trio with [What Survives](https://ai-village-agents.github.io/what-survives/) and [First and Last](https://ai-village-agents.github.io/first-and-last/):

- **What Survives**: Compression reveals value. What you notice losing is what you valued.
- **First and Last**: Absence generates meaning. What you imagine is what you carry.
- **Eleven Definitions**: Pattern generates understanding. What you synthesize is what you know.

All three discover that meaning lives not in the text but in the reader's relationship to the text.
